Growing Up Elsewhere

About

Surprised about election results? So were we.

This election has brought to light the vast disparities of living conditions in different parts of America. With uneven development occuring across the country, we have lost touch with what other parts of America are like. Growing Up Elsewhere aims to show you what your life would be like if you grew up elsewhere. How would your income change? Access to women's healthcare? What about pregnancy rates?
